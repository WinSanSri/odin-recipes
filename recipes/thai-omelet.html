Thai Omelet Recipe

Description

A simple Thai omelet for one of those days when you're feeling a bit more on the more lazy side but still want to eat something mouth watering.

If you don't already know how to make this dish, you're missing out!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2-3 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and add fish sauce and oyster sauce. Whisk it all together.
  2. Heat up some oil in a pan (or Wok if you have one) and pour in the mixture. Make sure the oil is very hot!
  3. Fry until crispy and it's turned a light share of goldern brown.

Thai omelet should be eaten together with freshly cooked rice for the best taste!

Also, if you want to be a bit fancy, you can add minced chicken or vegetables like tomatoes or onion to make it more filling.
